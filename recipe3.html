Momma Rosa's Potato Salad

This recipe was created by my mom's grandma. She used to make it all the time for my grandma. The recipe was home made and my mom still makes it today.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes (5lbs)
  • Eggs (4)
  • Green Onions (3)
  • Celery (3)
  • Real Mayonnaise
  • Mustard
  • Paprika
  • Salt
Final Product

This is what your potato salad should look like after you're finished making it.

Steps

  1. Start boiling water in a large pot
  2. Wash/peel the potatoes
  3. Cut the potatoes into chunky pieces
  4. Add to water
  5. Add 4 eggs
  6. Boil for 45 mins or until potatoes are tender
  7. Drain potatoes and eggs
  8. Let cool
  9. Add the potatoes to a large bowl
  10. Cut celery and green onions and add them to the bowl
  11. Add 4-6 tablespoons of real mayonnaise
  12. Add 1-2 tablespoons of mustard
  13. Add 1 tablespoon of salt
  14. Peel 2 eggs and chop finely
  15. Mix all ingredients together
  16. Peel 2 eggs and slice to add around top of the potato salad
  17. Sprinkle paprika on top
  18. Refrigerate 30-45 minutes
